# Nutriq — App Store Screenshots Plan

Apple shows up to 10 screenshots; the **first 3 do 80% of the work** (they're
what shows in search results). Required size: **6.7" iPhone = 1290 × 2796 px**
(you can also supply 6.5"; App Store Connect scales for most other sizes).

## The 6 screenshots (in order)

| # | Headline (on the frame) | Subcaption | App screen to capture |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Dinner, decided.** | AI plans your week around your goals & pantry | Home dashboard (with a meal plan + KPIs) |
| 2 | **A week of meals in one tap** | Pick what you need — AI fills the rest | Meal generation → the meal option cards |
| 3 | **Your cart, filled for pickup** | Every ingredient matched to real products | Shop tab with matched Kroger products |
| 4 | **Uses what you already have** | Scan your pantry, waste less, spend less | Pantry tab (with items + expiry chips) |
| 5 | **It learns what you love** | Rate meals — it gets sharper every week | Recipe card with the rating row |
| 6 | **Hit your goals, skip the math** | Calories & macros calculated for you | Goals tab (BMR card + weight trend chart) |

## Headline writing notes
- Keep headlines ≤ 5 words — they're read in a thumbnail.
- Lead with the benefit (decided, filled, learns), not the feature.
- #1 and #3 are your strongest — value prop + the killer differentiator
  (grocery pickup). If you only polish two, polish those.

## How to produce them
1. Build the app to your iPhone (or run in the iOS Simulator once Xcode is set
   up) and sign in to a demo account with some data populated.
2. Capture each screen above (Simulator: Cmd+S; device: side+volume-up).
3. Drop each capture into the matching branded frame in `marketing/screenshots/`
   (generated by `scripts/generate-screenshots.mjs`) — or send the 6 captures to
   Claude and I'll composite them into the frames for you.

## Demo account for Apple review
Apple reviewers need a working login. Create a demo account with a planned week
+ a few pantry items, and put the credentials in App Store Connect → App Review
Information. (Also have Kroger pre-connected if possible, or note in the review
notes that grocery features require a free Kroger account.)
