## Description (4000 char max)

**Nutriq turns "what's for dinner?" into a five-second decision.**

Most meal planners hand you recipes you don't want, for ingredients you don't have. Nutriq works backwards — from your real pantry, your goals, and the meals your family actually eats — then handles the grocery run for you.

**Plan around what you already own**
Scan items in or add them in seconds. Nutriq tracks what's in your pantry and what's about to expire, then builds meal plans that use it up first. Less waste, lower grocery bills.

**An AI planner that learns you**
Tell Nutriq how many breakfasts, lunches, and dinners you need this week and the cuisines you're in the mood for. Rate the meals you make — loved it, or never again — and every future plan gets sharper. It remembers your favorites, avoids the misses, and never repeats the same week twice.

**Set goals, hit them without the math**
Enter your weight, activity, and goal and Nutriq calculates your calorie and macro targets automatically (Mifflin-St Jeor). Every meal plan is built to fit them — whether you're losing, maintaining, or building.

**From plan to pickup in one tap**
Nutriq turns your week's meals into a smart shopping list, matches each ingredient to real products at your local Kroger, learns your preferred brands over time, and loads your pickup cart for you. No retyping. No forgotten items.

**Built for real households**
- Barcode scanner for instant pantry entry
- Expiry tracking so nothing rots in the back of the fridge
- Dietary preferences and allergy filters (keto, vegan, gluten-free, and more)
- Weekly preferences that change with your week
- Recipe cards with steps, prep time, and servings scaled to your household
- Weight logging and progress trends

**Free to start**
Plan up to 3 weeks free every month. Upgrade for unlimited planning, household sharing, and one-tap grocery ordering.

Nutriq. Feed your family better, without the mental math.
