Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Nutriq LLC ("Nutriq," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Nutriq mobile application and web application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the Service.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, set up your profile, or use features of the Service:

  • Account information — name, email address, and password
  • Profile information — household size, dietary preferences, allergies, and health goals
  • Health and body metrics — weight logs, calorie targets, and macro nutrition goals you enter voluntarily
  • Pantry data — food items, quantities, and expiration dates you add to the app
  • Meal data — meal plans you generate, meals you save, and ratings you provide
  • Shopping data — grocery lists and orders placed through connected grocery services
  • Usage data — features you interact with, actions you take, and frequency of use

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Service
  • Personalize your meal plan suggestions and recommendations
  • Generate AI-powered meal plans tailored to your household, goals, and pantry inventory
  • Process grocery orders through connected third-party services such as Kroger and Instacart
  • Send you service-related communications including expiry reminders and plan notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the Service and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

Artificial Intelligence and Your Data

Nutriq uses AI services, including Anthropic's Claude API, to generate meal plans and recipes. When you generate a meal plan, relevant information including your dietary preferences, health goals, and pantry contents is sent to our AI provider to generate your results. This data is transmitted securely and is subject to Anthropic's privacy practices. We do not send personally identifiable information such as your name or email to AI providers.

Aggregated and Anonymized Data

We may use anonymized, aggregated data — data that cannot reasonably be used to identify any individual — for research, product improvement, and business purposes including potential partnerships or acquisitions. We will never sell data that identifies you personally to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with third-party platforms to provide grocery ordering and other features. These include:

  • Supabase — database and authentication services
  • Anthropic — AI meal plan and recipe generation
  • Kroger — grocery product search and cart integration
  • Instacart — grocery ordering and delivery integration
  • Netlify — application hosting

When you connect a third-party grocery account, we store an access token to facilitate ordering on your behalf. We do not store your grocery account passwords. Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing their use of your data.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which employs row-level security ensuring users can only access their own data. All data transmission occurs over encrypted HTTPS connections. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app's Settings screen or by contacting us at the address below. Upon deletion, your personal data will be removed within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to export your data in a portable format
  • The right to object to certain processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). You have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information that identifies you. To exercise your California privacy rights, contact us at the address below.

Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
